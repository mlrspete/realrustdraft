FaQs

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please chat to our friendly team on discord or write us an email at info@realrust.gg

  • What is Real Rust?

    Real Rust is a premium anti-cheat system co-developed with industry leaders in gaming anti-cheat specifically for Rust. Our downloadable client monitors your Rust session from start to finish. Our server-side SDK collects the interactions between players and the game server while machine learning algorithms analyse the data in real-time to interpret them as frauds if abnormal behaviours are detected.

  • How do I join a server with Real Rust anti-cheat?

    Upon downloading and initiating the Real Rust client you simply sign-in with Steam and this will let the Real-Rust-enabled servers know your Steam account is running the anti-cheat. If at any point you close or sign out of the anti-cheat mid-session, the server you're connected to will be informed.

  • Will the Real Rust client slow down my PC?

    Our application runs without any impact on your game or device’s performances. Additionally, our client has a Windows Certificate and has been tested on many games with EAC.

  • What data does the Real Rust application use?

    We only collect data needed to interpret player interactions. The data is collected and is immediately encrypted to avoid privacy breaches. This data collected is related to the game window only. If you leave the game, the Real Rust application won’t be recording anything.

  • How is Real Rust better than other premium anti-cheat systems?

    Most current anti-cheat solutions focus on what’s installed on the computer rather than how players actually interact with the game. As a result, catching cheaters has become a constant game of cat and mouse, with the industry always chasing persistent and creative hackers. Our player-behaviour-based approach offers a non-invasive, proactive solution to detecting and preventing cheating in Rust.
